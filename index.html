About Us

About Us

Refuge Hill Ministry is a mission oriented ministry envisioned to be a light and take salvation to the ends of the Earth

For so the Lord has commanded us : I have set you as a light to the Gentiles, that you should be for salvation to the ends of the earth. (Acts 13:47).

With a burning passion for evangelism, discipleship, and church planting across villages, cities and Nations, our mission endeavors has reached Northern Nigeria and parts of Western states of Nigeria. We desire to see men saved, discover their purpose and walk in the fullness of their calling and ordination in God.

Our mission assignment to reach the unreached tribes and nations of the world and planting a viable church among them for their continuous growth is an utmost desire in our heart. We do not just engage in soul winning, but more importantly we emphasis discipleship. We believe that proper discipleship is the key to cultivating fruits that will last that is, committed and consistent believers in Christ.

Our current mission field is Ode-aye in Okitipupa Ondo State, and our 6-year labour has resulted in the salvation/discipleship of hundreds of souls to the glory of God.

We are committed to soul winning, proper grooming and discipleship of converts and raising men who are relevant in the end-time agenda of God either as a career missionary or in the market place.

Ode-Aye Mission Project

I am crying for a massive salvation of the land of Ode-Aye and that is your responsibility. These were the words of the Lord to us in July 2016. Intercession for the land began until 2019 when the Lord gave the marching order to set out.

Ode-Aye is a town among the Ikale tribe of Ondo State, Nigeria.

A church has been planted in Ode-Aye with a growing number of disciples who are also dedicated to seeing their land won for Jesus.

Leadership

Leadership

President

  Pastor Lanre Oyenola is the visionary leader of Refuge Hill Ministry, an evangelism and mission oriented ministry.
  He is an evangelist and a dynamic teacher of the word, with ministry experience which spans across several states in Nigeria. He is passionate about the Lord and the expansion of His kingdom. He is saddled with the responsibility of raising a strong mission minded army for the Lord especially among the youth.

Pastor Lanre is an alumnus of Calvary Ministry (Capro) School of Missions, Jos, Plateau State, a highly revered institute that has successfully trained about a thousand missionaries who are now serving the Lord across continents of the world.

Alongside his evangelistic work, he also serves as the lead pastor of the church arm of Refuge Hill Ministry, he ministers at various outreaches, crusades and conferences with tangible presence and power of the Holy Spirit.

He is the president of The Refuge Hill school of Ministry, an interdenominational school where believers are trained to skillfully and accurately present the gospel and be relevant in the end time move of God.

He is married to Pastor Gift Oyenola, a co-labourer with whom he began the ministry, they both serve hand-in-hand in expanding God's kingdom everywhere God instructs. Their marriage is blessed with two children.
